Waste Guide

Learn Proper Waste Segregation 🗑️

Dispose waste correctly to maximize recycling and minimize environmental harm.

GREEN BIN
🌿

Biodegradable

Kitchen waste, food scraps, vegetable peels, garden clippings, leaves — all items that naturally decompose.

BLUE BIN
♻️

Recyclable

Paper, cardboard, plastic bottles, glass jars, metal cans — clean and dry items that can be reprocessed.

SPECIAL HANDLING
⚠️

Hazardous

E-waste, batteries, chemicals, paint, medicines — items requiring certified handling and special disposal.

BLACK BIN
🗑️

General Waste

Non-recyclable, mixed waste that doesn't fit other categories — minimize this by reducing, reusing, recycling.
